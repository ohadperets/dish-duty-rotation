Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Dish Duty Rotation ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our web application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up using Google, Facebook, or email, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo (if provided).
  • Group Data: Information about the groups you create or join, including group names, member lists, and dish rotation history.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the app, such as features accessed and actions performed.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the dish rotation service
  • Manage your groups and track dish duty assignments
  • Authenticate your account and ensure security
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send you important notifications about your groups

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which employs industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and user permissions

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data:

  • With members of your groups (group names, rotation history)
  • With service providers (Google Firebase) to operate our service
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Third-Party Authentication

When you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive limited information from these services as per their privacy policies. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to create and authenticate your account.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection

8. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please visit our Data Deletion page or contact us at support@dishess.com.

Upon request, we will delete:

  • Your account information
  • Your profile data
  • Your group memberships (you will be removed from all groups)
  • Your personal dish rotation history

Note: Group data created by you may remain if other members are still active in those groups.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@dishess.com
  • Website: https://dishess.com
