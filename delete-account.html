Data Deletion Timeline

Once we receive your deletion request:

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated within 24 hours
  • 7 days: All personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems
  • 30 days: Backup copies will be purged from our recovery systems

What Happens to Your Groups?

Groups you created:

  • If you're the only member: The group will be deleted
  • If other members exist: The group remains active, but you'll be removed
  • Group history will be preserved for remaining members

Groups you joined:

  • You'll be removed from all groups
  • Your name will be removed from future rotations
  • Historical records may retain your name for audit purposes

Alternatives to Account Deletion

If you're having issues or just want a break, consider these alternatives:

  • Leave specific groups: You can leave groups without deleting your account
  • Change your email: Update your contact information in settings
  • Contact support: We're happy to help resolve any issues
